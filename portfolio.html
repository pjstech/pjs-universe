📂 HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS

1
Upload to GitHub
Go to your GitHub repo → click "Add file" → "Upload files" → drag your photo into the photos/track/ or photos/builds/ folder → commit.
2
Edit portfolio.html
In GitHub, click portfolio.html → click the pencil (edit) icon → find a placeholder comment and replace it with the img tag below.
3
Save & It's Live
Scroll down → click "Commit changes" → your photo appears on the live site within 1–2 minutes. Repeat for every photo.
Replace a placeholder div with this (track photos): <div class="gallery-item"> <img src="photos/track/your-photo.jpg" alt="Race car" draggable="false" oncontextmenu="return false;"> <div class="overlay"><span class="overlay-text">Your caption</span></div> </div> For build photos (use "square" class): <div class="gallery-item square"> <img src="photos/builds/your-build.jpg" alt="Scale build" draggable="false" oncontextmenu="return false;"> <div class="overlay"><span class="overlay-text">Build name</span></div> </div>

💡 Photo tip: Rename your files without spaces — use hyphens instead. E.g. bmw-m3-gt-2024.jpg not BMW M3 GT 2024.jpg. Keep file sizes under 2MB for fast loading.
