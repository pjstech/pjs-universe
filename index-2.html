The Story Behind The Brands

WHO IS PJ?

Cars have been my obsession since I could hold a paintbrush. I'm a professional scale model builder — the kind who notices panel gap consistency and loses sleep over a shade of red that's 2% too orange.

But my love for cars doesn't stay on the workbench. It takes me to the racetrack, where I photograph the real cars before recreating them in miniature. That connection between the real and the scale is what makes everything I do unique.

Why Only 3 Per Year?

Each build gets my full, undivided attention. I don't rush. I don't compromise. Three builds a year means every client gets the obsessive detail work that makes a PJ's Scaleworks piece genuinely one of a kind.

What Determines the Price?

Pricing depends on the complexity of the livery, custom parts required, travel distance to the event, and specific client requirements. Every quote is personalised — no two builds are the same.
