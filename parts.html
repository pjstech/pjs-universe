📂 HOW TO ADD PART PHOTOS

Upload your part photos to GitHub, then replace the placeholder divs in parts.html with img tags.

1. Upload photo to GitHub → photos/parts/ folder 2. In parts.html find a placeholder div like this: <div class="part-photo"><div class="ph">...</div></div> 3. Replace it with: <div class="part-photo"> <img src="photos/parts/your-part.jpg" alt="Part description" draggable="false" oncontextmenu="return false;"> </div>

💡 Keep photo filenames short, no spaces. E.g. 3d-wing-mirror.jpg, aluminium-wheels-bmw.jpg
